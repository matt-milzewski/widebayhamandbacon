Our Story

For over 40 years, Wide Bay Ham & Bacon has been a cornerstone of the Fraser Coast community. Our family-run butcher shop continues the tradition of crafting premium smallgoods using the founder's original wood-smoke recipe.

What started as a small local business has grown into an award-winning establishment, known for our commitment to quality and traditional curing methods. We take pride in sourcing our pork from Queensland producers and processing everything in-house.

Our dedication to quality has earned us a 4.9★ rating from our loyal customers, who appreciate our attention to detail and commitment to traditional methods.
